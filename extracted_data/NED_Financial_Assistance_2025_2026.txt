TITLE:
Financial Assistance / Scholarship – 2025-2026 (Fresh Cases)

CONTENT:
All deserving students from First Year of Engineering, BS, and Architecture departments who intend to avail financial assistance or scholarship on a Need-cum-Merit basis are advised to submit the prescribed application form along with relevant documents.

Applications must be submitted through the respective Class Advisor or Chairperson to the Department of Students’ Affairs.

The deadline for submission is 18-09-2025.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
1. Mark certificates of SSC, HSC, and B.Sc (if applicable).
2. Salary certificate of father or guardian, if employed.
3. Updated pension book of father or guardian, if retired.
4. In case of business, an affidavit for income verification (original).
5. National Identity Card or B-Form of all dependents.
6. University Identity Card.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Incomplete applications will not be entertained.
- Students admitted under the Self Finance category are not eligible for this scholarship.
